2.3 Troubleshooting Connection

Lost or hanging connection:

1 - Refresh page 2 - Close the Google Chrome tab then reopen it. (You might want to copy the URL, also you will have to reconnect to the kit after you reopen the page) 3 - Check WiFi connection 4 - Turn the kit Off then back On. Reconnect.

Notes:

You are not connected to the internet when you connect to the EveBrain network if it says limited connection everything is okay!

Please use Google Chrome for best results

If you lose connection refresh the page.
